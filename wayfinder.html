Northern General Hospital Wayfinder

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL

Created for module in my second year is dedicated to group software development. In groups of 6 which included both Software Engineering and Computer Science students, we had to produce a prototype for a chosen client. Our client was the NHS, specifically Northern General Hospital. Their requirements were simple, to produce an application that could direct patients around the huge hospital. We had one week to produce this. We used an agile development process, Scrum, to manage our time and work as a team and chose to create a web application, specifically for mobile use. By the end of the week we demonstrated our product to the NHS client who was impressed and asked if they could get in contact to use some of our ideas to incorporate into their own application. Our application relies on QR codes that are placed at doors, staircases and lifts around the building.
